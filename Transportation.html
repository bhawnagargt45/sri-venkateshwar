Transportation in 2050

The majority of vehicles on the road could be self-driving by 2050, according to enthusiasts for electric and autonomous cars. They believe technology, plus the push for cleaner air will be the incentive for big changes in the way we get from A to B. This is part of the BBC's Disruptors series. electric vehicles will outnumber gas-powered, which have been outlawed from city limits due to global warming. Because of new breakthroughs in making cars more intelligent, lighter and stronger, more people are choosing to commute longer distances than ever before.
